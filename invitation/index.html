The Rituals

Ceremonies We Perform

01
Vradham
A quiet ritual observed the day before the wedding, where both families offer prayers to their ancestors and observe a fast, seeking blessings for the couple's new life together.
02
Nichayathartham
The formal engagement where both families come together to bless the match, exchange gifts, and announce the wedding date in front of family and elders.
03
Reception
An evening of celebration where Vishal & Pooja welcome friends and extended family to share in their joy, with dinner and festivities.
04
Kasi Yatra
A playful, symbolic moment where the groom pretends to set off toward Kasi to live a simple, ascetic life until the bride's father steps in and convinces him that married life is the better path.
05
Oonjal
The couple is seated together on a flower-decked swing, gently rocked back and forth while the women of the family sing — a reminder to stay balanced together through life's ups and downs.
06
Mangalya Dharanam
The heart of the wedding ; the groom ties the sacred thaali around the bride's neck at the precise auspicious moment, as the nadaswaram plays and the couple is showered with blessings.
07
Agni Pradakshinam (Saptapadi)
The couple circles the sacred fire together, taking seven steps as the priest chants vedic verses where each step a vow, for nourishment, strength, prosperity, and a lifelong bond witnessed by Agni itself.
08
Grahapravesam
The bride's first entrance into her new home welcomed with an aarti by her mother-in-law, stepping in right foot first as a gesture of good fortune for the household.
09
Nalangu
A lighthearted, fun-filled ritual where the newly married couple play games and tease each other gently. The perfect ice-breaker after a day full of solemn vows.
